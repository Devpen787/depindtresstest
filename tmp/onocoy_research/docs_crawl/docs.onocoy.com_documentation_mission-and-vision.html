Mission and Vision

Mission

  • onocoy builds the densest GNSS reference station network in the world by leveraging Web3 technology to provide reference stations at a scale and density only possible through a community-driven approach.

  • onocoy’s system is resilient and reliable due to decentralization and shared stakeholder governance.

  • onocoy aggregates and shares reference station networks across the industry to meet the price points and ease of use needed for mass adoption.

  • By utilizing smart contracts, the platform and value chain is automated, hence it is efficient and transparent.

Vision

  • We at onocoy strongly advocate for the inclusive accessibility and utilization of GNSS high precision technology for all.

  • We make the system community owned, decentralized and transparently ensuring trust, making it an indispensable utility for the world.

  • We pledge that our services are available across borders, and without discrimination, now and for the future.
