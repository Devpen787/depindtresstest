Hardware recommendations

You will need a GNSS receiver and antenna in order to be able to mine rewards. onocoy is hardware-agnostic, meaning it can work with any type of receiver and antenna, provided they meet the technical requirements.

Onocoy does not sell its own hardware. Here are a few recommendations from our partners:

  • Supports GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, BeiDou, QZSS and NavIC

  • Wifi connection

Some remarks if you want to DIY or bring your own:

  • For maximizing rewards, a triple- or quad band GNSS receiver that supports all of the major constellations (GPS, Galileo, Beidou and Glonass) is required.

  • GNSS receivers with a lower number of supported frequency bands (e.g. only L1+L2, or L1+L5) will receive rewards, albeit at a lower level than triple/quad band receivers

  • A high quality GNSS antenna which supports the receiver’s frequency bands.

  • The GNSS receiver should either directly support connection to an Ethernet/Wifi network, or be connected to a host (Raspberry Pi, ESP32, or equivalent) that acts as a bridge.

  • The receiver must have "NTRIP Server" functionality.

  • Please note that GNSS receivers only receive signals, hence, there is no additional electromagnetic transmission originating from operating the receiver.
