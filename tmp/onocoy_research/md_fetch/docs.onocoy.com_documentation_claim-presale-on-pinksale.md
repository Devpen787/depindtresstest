onocoy held its token presale on PinkSale in July 2025. Here are the instructions how to claim your tokens from the presale.

## How to Claim

### Manually Claim ONO Tokens to Your Wallet <a href="#h.z1j5ibjat0rw" id="h.z1j5ibjat0rw"></a>

As a recipient, you can easily withdraw your unlocked **ONO tokens** from a vesting or payment contract directly to your wallet. 10% of the $ONOs from the presale will be unlocked at TGE, and from then on 1% is unlocked every day. Don't worry, you don't have to claim every day, whenever you claim you will get the amount that has built up.

**Note**: You have to expect a fee from the provider of 0.01 SOL per claim. Once the contract has completed, all remaining ONO tokens will be automatically claimed to the recipientâ€™s wallet.
