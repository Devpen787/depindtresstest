DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network) is a web3-powered
model where physical infrastructure is:

- 

  Built and maintained by a distributed community,

- 

  Incentivized through tokens,

- 

  Coordinated via blockchain and smart contracts.

Instead of centralized companies owning and operating the
infrastructure, anyone can contribute to the network, earn rewards, and
help scale it globally.
