Create and connect a wallet

A cryptocurrency wallet (or crypto wallet for short) is a tool -
software or hardware - that allows you to allows you to store, send, and
receive cryptocurrencies like ONOs and others.

A crypto wallet serves the following purposes:

- 

  Stores your private keys – these are secret codes that give you access
  to your cryptocurrency.

- 

  Manages your public keys and addresses – used to receive funds from
  others.

- 

  Lets you send transactions – authorizing the movement of your crypto
  using your private key.
