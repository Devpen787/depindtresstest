1.  6. How to Contribute.

Partners

Deployment partners:

- 

  Provides all the hardware required to create a base station like: GNSS
  receiver, GNSS antenna, cabling, Router.

- 

  Sets up and configures all the aforementioned equipment.

- 

  Provides a suitable location to install the GNSS antenna and receiver.

- 

  Resells the GNSS raw data in RTCM format to onocoy.

Hardware partners:

onocoy is working together with hardware partners to make sure that
miners can get recommended base station receivers.

Hardware partners offer GNSS hardware, like receivers, antennas and
cabling and have a website detailing their products, pricing, and
support documentation. They also provide direct support to their
hardware customers.

Rooftop partners:

Rooftop partners provide locations, specifically rooftops, suitable for
installing GNSS base stations. They are responsible for managing these
locations and acquiring the necessary hardware for the base station
installations.

Distribution partners:

Distribution partners buy hardware from hardware partners and resell it
to individuals, institutes, and companies. Additionally, they can resell
data to end data users.
