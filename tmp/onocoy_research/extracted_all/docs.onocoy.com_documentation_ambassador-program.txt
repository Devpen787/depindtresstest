Ambassador Program

Welcome to the onocoy Ambassador Program ‚Äî our way of empowering early
contributors who want to support the future of decentralized positioning
infrastructure (DePIN). This program activates passionate community
members across three key verticals: Tech, Brand, and Community.

Purpose

The Ambassador Program is designed to:

- 

  Grow a trusted group of contributors across technical, marketing, and
  community areas.

- 

  Increase awareness, credibility, and traction in the DePIN and Web3
  infra ecosystems.

- 

  Reward high-value contributors with recognition, tokens, and early
  access.

We use a tiered system with defined roles, tracked KPIs, and a
structured onboarding process. Entry is open but selective: we
prioritize commitment, quality, and alignment.

What We Provide

All onocoy Ambassadors receive access to the resources, tools, and
support they need to succeed in their role:

- 

  Templates & Assets: Co-branded visuals, guide templates, and content
  resources.

- 

  Tracking Tools: KPI submission forms, dashboards, and performance
  logs.

- 

  Communication Channels: Group chats with the core team and fellow
  ambassadors.

- 

  Guides & Documentation: Onboarding scripts, FAQs, and moderation
  guidelines (as applicable).

- 

  Platform Access: Role-specific permissions on Discord, X/Twitter
  tools, and analytics trackers.

Additional role-specific materials (like setup verification or referral
links) are shared during onboarding

Ambassador Journey

All new participants begin with a 4-week trial period as Ambassadors.
During this time, their activity, contributions, and overall engagement
are monitored through tracked KPIs. After successfully completing this
trial with consistent performance, they become eligible for promotion to
Senior Ambassador. Senior Ambassadors take on greater responsibilities,
including managing task groups, reviewing submissions, mentoring new
Ambassadors, and participating in early strategy discussions and beta
testing. Advancement is based on demonstrated leadership, initiative,
high-quality work, and a positive overall impact.
